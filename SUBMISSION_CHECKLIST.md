# NANDAHack Submission Checklist

## Phase 1 — 20%

- Register using the participation form.
- Complete your separate NANDA Town improvement.
- Use a branch named `hackathon/your-name-topic`.
- Add a test.
- Run `make ci-local`.
- Open the pull request on `projnanda/nandatown`.

Your earlier transport-latency improvement belongs here, not inside this SafeRoute repository.

## Phase 2 — 80%

- Push SafeRoute to GitHub.
- Deploy it to Render, Railway, Vercel, or Fly; do not use a laptop tunnel.
- Confirm `/health`, `/plan`, and `/skill.md` work on the public URL.
- Set `PUBLIC_BASE_URL` to the final public URL.
- Submit the skill on the NANDA Town skills page.
- Fill in your bare GitHub username so Phase 1 and Phase 2 can be matched.
- Confirm the card appears and the badge says `link responded`.

## Final form and video

- Record the Phase 2 demo.
- Complete or resubmit the final Google form.
- Add the video link.

## Deadlines in Eastern Time

- Initial submission and final Phase 1 PR: Friday, July 10, 2026 at 12:00 PM ET.
- Phase 2 edits, required video, and final Google form: Saturday, July 11, 2026 at 2:00 PM ET.
