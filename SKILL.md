# SafeRoute AI

## [Open SafeRoute AI](https://saferoute-travel-agent.onrender.com)

> **Disaster-aware travel guidance for safer decisions during emergencies.**

---

## What It Does

SafeRoute AI creates personalized travel safety plans using:

- Traveler location
- Transportation method
- Mobility requirements
- Children, pets, elderly travelers, or medical needs

---

## How It Works

```text
Traveler enters location
          ↓
SafeRoute checks the request
          ↓
Risk assessment is generated
          ↓
Personalized actions are returned
          ↓
Traveler follows official guidance
